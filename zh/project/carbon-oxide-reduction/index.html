12. Photoelectrocatalytic Reduction of CO$_{2}$ to Paraffin Using P-N Heterojunctions (Times cited = 6)

Jinyuan Wang, Yongji Guan, Xiaogang Yu, Youzhi Cao, Jiazang Chen, Yilin Wang, Bin Hu, Huanwang Jing, iScience, 2020, 23(1), 100768-100768
Dec 12, 2019 iScience
Here we show that a series of three-dimensional C/N-doped heterojunctions of Zn$_{x}$:Co$_{y}$@Cu are successfully fabricated and applied as photocathodes in the PEC reduction of CO$_{2}$ to generate paraffin product.
