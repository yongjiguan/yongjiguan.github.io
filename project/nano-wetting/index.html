4. Dynamic Three-Dimensional Nanowetting Behavior of Imidazolium-Based Ionic Liquids Probed by Molecular Dynamics Simulation (Times cited = 20)

Yongji Guan, Qunfeng Shao, Wenqiong Chen, Shimin Liu, Xiaoping Zhang, Youquan Deng, The Journal of Physical Chemistry C, 2017, 121(42), 23716-23726

Dynamic nanowetting behavior of three ionic liquids nanodroplets with radii between 10 and 30 Å on silicon surface is probed by molecular dynamics simulation from 300 to 500 K.
