Flow-Induced Energy Harvesting

Yongji Guan, Wenqiong Chen, Shimin Liu, Xiaoping Zhang, and Youquan Deng
PDF Project

Abstract

Herein, we employed MD simulation to investigate the generation of flow-induced voltage through driving ILs over graphene nano-channel. The flow-induced voltage can be potentially applied in nanoelectromechanical systems and offer much promise for bio-molecular sensing and bio-medical fields.
